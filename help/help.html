1. Task Management (Digital Kanban Board)

The digital Kanban board helps you organize and manage tasks efficiently. Here's how it works:

  • Drag tasks between columns to indicate their status (To Do, In Progress, Done).
  • Add new tasks by clicking the "Add Task" button.
  • Set deadlines, priority levels, and assign tasks to yourself or others.

2. Mood Tracker

The mood tracker allows you to track your emotional state throughout the day. Follow these steps:

  • Check-in hourly by selecting your mood (Happy, Neutral, Sad, etc.).
  • View insights based on your mood trends over time.

3. Notifications

The app will send notifications for tasks that are overdue. You can:

  • Receive reminders when tasks are close to their deadlines.
  • Mark tasks as completed or reschedule them as needed.

4. Links to Professional Help

If you're feeling overwhelmed, the app offers quick access to professional support:

  • Click on the "Get Help" button to access resources and contact a counselor.
  • Explore mental health services tailored for workplace wellness.

5. Gamification

The app includes a gamification system to motivate you:

  • Earn points for completing tasks.
  • Compete with colleagues or track your progress over time.

6. Mindful Activity Reminders

The app will remind you to take short breaks and practice mindfulness:

  • Receive hourly reminders for breathing exercises or stretching.
  • Follow guided mindfulness activities to reduce stress.
